Marketplace solutions are increasingly giving customers opportunities to get fast solutions both in terms of timing and costs. Studies suggest that there is a price differential from forwarders of 41% in quotes for the same shipment.


LogistCompare is a warehouse marketplace platform aiming to support both retailers and warehouse providers :


Customers looking for storage space

  • Compares rates at the touch of a button.
  • Provide full cost breakdown with every quotation. 
  • Gives access to “chat” buttons for a seamless communication with warehouse providers. 
  • Provide instant users reviews.   
  • Offer an easy and secure booking system.
     

 Warehouse Providers 

  •  Provides the feasibility to rent out space online. 
  •  Allows good customer reviews to improve market positioning.
  •  Promotes services.
  • Increases visibility in the market.
