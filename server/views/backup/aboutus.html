About Us

LogistCompare is an innovative marketplace for warehouse space and services. 

We connect  organisations that need storage space with warehouse providers that can accommodate their requirements. Our platform allows warehouse providers to rent out available space online and to promote their services.

Our vision is to link logistics, technology and market demands to proactively meet your customer expectations.

  • Receive a full cost breakdown and availability.  
    No time wasted in benchmarking quotes
  • Read reviews of warehouses made by other customers.  
    Less worries about reliability of their services
  • Interact directly with the warehouses.
    No time wasted in waiting for middlemen.
